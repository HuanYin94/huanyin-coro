Huan Yin

Dr. Huan Yin

 

Professor, Hunan University

 

Email: huanyin@hnu.edu.cn; zjuyinhuan@gmail.com

 

Huan Yin received the B.Eng. and Ph.D. degrees from Zhejiang University (ZJU), at Hangzhou, in 2016 and 2021, respectively. He was a visiting scholar at University of Technology Sydney (UTS). After graduation, he worked at National University of Singapore (NUS) as a Research Fellow. Then he joined the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) as a Research Assistant Professor. He is joining the School of AI and Robotics, Hunan University (HNU), as a Professor. His research interests include robotics, SLAM, robot perception and robot learning.
